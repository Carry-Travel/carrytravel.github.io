Teams benefit the most from using Carry

Skip the reimbursements. Everything is booked through the corporate account but employees still get their miles

No more reimbursement reports and telling the Manager how much you spend. Everything is automatically expensed to the corporate account. Managers receive a dashboard to view employee spend and make adjustments as necessary. Carry knows your employer's travel policy and books the best possible travel under that policy. No stress.

We save time for both managers and employees. Never again will either side look at an expense report. Managers are auatomtically alerted when a transaction requires attention (but we know this rarely happens). Smarter expensing for everyone. End users keep track of all their miles and points all in one dashboard. We redeem the points for you so that you don't have to go through the hassle.

Tailored Just for You

We care about the details so that you don't have to

For the first ~18 years of your life, your parents booked all your travel. They were your personal assistant for all things travel related, and they were pros. Now you're the parent and you have to book travel for your kids, or you book travel for youself. Let us get to know you as well as your parents do, and make personalized travel decisions for both you and your family. Never again stress about booking travel for you or your family.

We know which airlines you have elite status with. We know which airports you prefer for layovers. We know what time of day you like to travel, where you live, where you work...okay it's getting a bit creepy. Our secure servers and anonymized support allow us to know everything about you except your personal identifiers. We automatically learn which trips are for work and which trips should be billed to your personal account. Family reunion in Alaska? We'll book flights for everyone on the personal account using miles. Business trip in New York? We'll book business class on the corporate account.

Services on Day of Travel

From door to door, we've got you covered

Carry doesn't just prioritize your flight plan. Travel takes up a lot of head space. If you've ever spent time deciding how early to leave for the airport, thinking about traffic, or been unsure about security wait times, then forget about all of those hassles. Carry will tell you by when to leave the house, and even wake you up in the morning if you want. Carry can order your car, avoid traffic, tell you which terminals have the quickest security line, and leave time for your favorite breakfast spot.

Carry knows whether you want to check in to your hotel early or check out late. You will never have to make your own phone calls or talk to a customer service rep. ever again. Let Carry order those extra towels or toothbrush that you forgot. Carry has aleady paid for parking for your rental car, never stress about travel decisions again.

No Cancellations or Delays

We don't tell you about cancellations because we've already rebooked you on a better flight

We specialize in dealing with airline cancellations or delays. We'll rebook your flight and call your airline for you. No wait time, no delay. We won't even brag about solving your problems. You get from point A -> B, let us do the leg work. Missed your flight? Let us know, we'll take care of it, and we'll forgive you too. Never wait on the phone again with customer service. Our AI does the waiting and our 24/7 team sweet talks customer service. We'll pay for the cost of your flight change if it comes to it.

Ended up paying for a checked bag? Text us the receipt and we'll get it reimbursed through your company's Concur automatically.

We are currently only for teams

If you think your team could benefit from our product, let us know! We have a small private beta for individuals who travel a lot. Let us know how we can help!
