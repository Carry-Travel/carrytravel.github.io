FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Why is this better than booking my own travel?

We save you both time and money. Our personalized recommendation engine will find better travel options than you or your mom. We manage the expense report for you, and we're reachable 24/7 within seconds.

How much does this cost?

Depending on the size of your team, you'll pay a small service fee on each transaction, but we will always save you more money than we charge, otherwise our service is free.

Why does this exist only for teams? I want it now!

We can provide a lot more value to teams in the initial release of our product. Once we build more and more automation we're excited to offer Carry to everyone!

How do you guys deliver my itinerary so quickly?

Because you give us access to your calendar, we precompute itineraries for your potential trips. Our magical software can give instant travel plans for spontaneous plans too. We make sure that we gather all your data upfront so that we can offer single click booking for any trip.
